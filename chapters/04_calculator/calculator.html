Quick Examples

Built-in Functions

sqrt(x)
Square root
sqrt(16) → 4
abs(x)
Absolute value
abs(-7) → 7
floor(x)
Round down to integer
floor(3.9) → 3
ceil(x)
Round up to integer
ceil(3.1) → 4
round(x)
Round to nearest integer
round(3.6) → 4
max(a,b,…)
Maximum of arguments
max(3,1,7,2) → 7
min(a,b,…)
Minimum of arguments
min(3,1,7,2) → 1
log(x)
Natural logarithm
log(1) → 0
